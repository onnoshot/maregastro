Our Story

The soul of Lake Sapanca,
plated

In that singular moment when the pines meet the water and the morning mist drifts gently across the lake, a dream took root in Didi Otel's garden, woven seamlessly into nature.

From its light to its tables, from each plate to its very soul, every detail of Mare Gastro was envisioned, planned and brought to life with the utmost care by Kadir Çiçek within Didi Otel.

At the helm of the kitchen stands Executive Chef Doğan Anapa, who turns a background in cinema into flavour. He brings the purest ingredients of the Aegean and the Mediterranean to the plate as though telling a story.

✦
Kadir Çiçek Didi Otel · Concept, Design & Realisation
Our Chef

Cooking, much like making a film, is a way of telling a story.

The kitchen unites and shares; it is a space brimming with human stories.

Our plates reflect our state of mind.
